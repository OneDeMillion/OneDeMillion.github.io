Target Audience

The target audience for the Sunblink Forecast website is individuals ranging from 16-66 years-old. They come from all genders, race, income and education levels. They are drawn to nature and enjoying outdoor activities and health-connected recreation.

Personas

Nicole and Mark are in their mid-30's. They have four children and love spending time outside, exploring, playing and keeping in shape. They waterski, snowboard, fish, hike and snowmobile. They look for new adventures. Mark is a foot doctor and Nicole has her own fitness business. They work hard and play hard. They want to encourage a healthy lifestyle for their children. They take calculated risks, but would never put their chilren in jeapordy. Mark has offices throughout the northern part of the state and watches the weather closely for travel conditions. Most weekends they travel, in or out of state and need up to date weather conditions.
