Weather Ideas for Science Homeschool Projects

by Kim DeMille

With the majority of us suddenly turned into full-time educators for children, we may be finding that creativity is the key to keeping our children's attention and excitement about their studies. If that's the case for you, we have provided you a few ideas for Weather Activities that would fit the bill for any Science Projects that you might need to complete, and they have the bonus of being fun too!

Exploring weather phenomena through experiments and other activities can help make the science come alive for students and help them really understand the atmosphere and the weather within it. And let's not forget, they can be fun for the whole family to do.

  • Weather Around the World from Mama Smiles
  • Cloud Simulation from Adventures in Mommydom
  • What Causes Storms? from Frogs and Fairies
  • Tornado in a Bottle Science Experiment from Edventures with Kids
  • What Makes the Wind Blow and Wind Vocabulary from Left Brain Craft Brain
  • The Science of Condensation and Frost from Schooling a Monkey
  • Cloud Science from The Happy Housewife
