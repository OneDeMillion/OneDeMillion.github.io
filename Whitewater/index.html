Let's talk about Salmon Whitewater Adventures....

We, at Salmon White Adventures, are dedicated to providing the most personalized whitewater rafting experience we can offer. Each and every employee that you interact with knows that what we offer, is not just a rafting trip, but a step out into the best places in the world. The Salmon River, Idaho! We LOVE rafting. We are completely passionate about it and feel you will be too once you have experienced Whitewater rafting on the stunning Salmon River. It allows you to be more connected to our beautiful rivers, our environment, and our state of mind. We believe that a day on the river is the greatest gift you can give yourself, your family, coworker, teammate, friend, or anyone else whom you would like to bring along on a journey down the river. Young or old, first timer or experienced rafter, Salmon Whitewater Adventures has the river excursion for you, and we, along with our professional River Guides, are dedicated to making this your “BEST DAY EVER”.

We offer three exciting adventure packages for you to choose from...

  • Family Rafting Trip
  • 2-Day Kayak Trip
  • 5-Day Run The River Wild
